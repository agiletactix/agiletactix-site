## What is Career Optional?

Career Optional is the podcast for professionals who want to stop depending on a single employer for their livelihood.

Whether you're a corporate professional building yourself into a one-person powerhouse using AI inside your current role, or an aspiring solo builder creating your own business with AI as a co-founder — this show is for you.

**The core thesis:** The same skill set — agile thinking + AI automation — works whether you stay corporate or go indie. Career optionality, not job security, is the goal.

## Who is Danny Liu?

In January 2023, Danny was one of over 1,000 Scrum Masters eliminated in a single layoff wave. Two weeks later, he had a new Fortune 500 role — not because of certifications, but because he could build systems that delivered results.

Now he teaches those exact skills at [AgileTactix](https://agiletactix.ai).

## What to Expect

Story-driven episodes exploring the intersection of agile methodology, AI automation, and career resilience. No weekly news commentary. Real stories, real frameworks, real results.
